Celeberating 77th Republic Day

We cordially invite you to join us in celebrating the 77th Republic Day of India with pride and patriotism.
The event will include flag hoisting, the National Anthem, cultural performances, and inspiring speeches highlighting the values of our Constitution and democracy.
Your presence will make the occasion more meaningful as we come together to honor our nation and its spirit of unity.

We look forward to your gracious presence.

Date : 26th January 2026

Venue : Main Ground, Graphic Era deemed to Be University, Clement Town, Dehradun

For More Details Visit GEU Official Website

The 77th Republic Day celebration will begin with the grand flag hoisting ceremony, followed by the National Anthem and an impressive NCC parade.
Students will present cultural performances including patriotic songs, dance, and short skits reflecting the spirit of India.
Esteemed faculty members and guests will address the gathering highlighting the importance of the Constitution and national unity.
